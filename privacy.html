Privacy & Cookies

Last updated: August 13, 2025

Tercarioli uses web analytics to improve the site experience. Analytics is only activated if you give your consent. No ads are shown and no advertising profiles are created.

Controller

Data controller: Tercarioli (Jorge Tercarioli).
Contact: jorge@tercarioli.com.

What data is collected and why

Analytics (Google Analytics 4): measurement of visits, page views, and interaction with buttons (for example, clicks on “Start now”). The legal basis is your consent.

When accepting analytics, Google may set cookies associated with GA4 (e.g., _ga, _ga_*) to distinguish users and sessions. If you do not accept, Consent Mode is used and no analytics cookies are set.

Providers and transfers

Analytics is a service provided by Google LLC. See their official documentation and policies. This site is hosted by Vercel. When you click “Start now,” you will be directed to Usequeue/Stripe to manage checkout; those sites apply their own privacy policies.

Retention

Analytics data is retained for the period configured in GA4. You can revoke your consent at any time.

Your rights

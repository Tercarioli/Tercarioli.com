NIKE’S 2025
OLIMPIA KIT
PRIDETAG

Football kits have always been about identity. The pridetag, a small symbol hidden near the collar, carries that spirit, and for Nike’s 2025 official kit for Club Olimpia, it was mine: an original illustration of El Viejo Decano, raising three fingers to honor the club’s three Copa Libertadores titles.

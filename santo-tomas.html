CULTURAL
CONVERSATIONS
OUTPERFORM
PRODUCT TALK.

New yerba mate in a flat category. Product talk adds nothing—everyone knows mate; no one needs it explained. The job wasn’t to teach the ritual, it was to respect it. So we saved our oxygen for culture.

We gave Santo Tomás a seat at the table: the first pour, the pass, the pause that keeps friends and families together. No tasting notes, no lectures—just the moments people already live. That’s how a new brand entered an old tradition: by joining the conversation, not trying to dominate it. And the campaign was recognized as the Best Multimedia Campaign of 2023.
